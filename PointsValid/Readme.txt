These files contain the determination of times series of presence absence of water in the validation points. 
Landsat_data_points_validation.xls contain time series extracted from GEE using the old version of the shapefile of validation points (149 pts)
Landsat_data_points_validation_complet contain times series with all points 273 points (used in AR PhD thesis)
Same comments for PTSinfos and PTSinfos_complet and TSPTS and TSPTS_complet. 

For checking, old and "complet" files are kept for comparison.
